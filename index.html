About Us

YouthRise Tunisia is a non-profit organization founded by young Tunisians passionate about reducing unemployment and empowering youth through education, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

We design training programs, mentorship opportunities, and partnerships that enable young people to build meaningful careers and contribute to Tunisia’s future.

Our Programs

Job Readiness & Training

We equip youth with essential skills: resume writing, communication, and digital literacy — empowering them for the workforce.

Entrepreneurship Incubator

Our incubator helps young Tunisians turn ideas into startups through mentorship, resources, and small grants.

Mentorship & Networking

We connect youth with professionals and entrepreneurs who provide guidance, inspiration, and career advice.
